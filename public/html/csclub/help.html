1. Introduction

Here is a quick refresher on some of the main responsibilities as office staff.

Good luck - your S23 OM.

2. Membership

Here is the rough gameplan if someone asks you about membership.

[Them]
Hi, I am looking to buy a CSC membership
[You]
Have you signed up for CSC before?
[ Yes ] [ No ] [ Maybe ]
  • If they say [YES] (Previous Member), then continue to renewing members.
  • If they say [NO] (New Member), then continue to signing people up.
  • If maybe, you can check if someone is a member in ceo.

2.1. Signing People Up

Here, you need to do 2 things before taking their money.

  1. Ask to see their watcard.
  2. Have them sign the Machine Usage Agreement
[Them]
I want to sign up for CSC
[You]
Great! Do you have your watcard on you?
[ Give WatCard ] [ Leave ]

You will need to confirm their identity in 2 ways:

  1. Make sure the photo on the card is them
  2. Later on when you register them in ceo make sure that the name that is autofilled matches the name on their watcard (the first name in ceo will be their legal name or preferred name, if they have one).
  3. You may return their watcard after the ceo step is completed.
[You]
Ok, before I sign you up, I need you to agree to our Machine Usage Agreement. Since CSC provides many computer resouces that you can use, we have a few rules that you need to agree to. Here is a quick summary:
  • Don't share your account with anyone.
  • Don't hog/abuse resources. There is more information about this on our wiki, wiki.csclub.uwaterloo.ca. And if you have further questions, ask Syscom.
  • Don't mine crypto.
  • Don't do anything illegal. No pirating, sharing copyrighted content, etc.
  • CSC Resources are for personal/course work only. This means you cannot be hosting company websites, or running your startup idea here.
If you want more information, go to: [ csclub.ca > Resources > Machine Usage Agreement ]
[ Agree and Sign MUA ] [ Give up ]

Once the user signs, you will also have sign and date below their signature. Next, you have to take payment.

2.2. Renewing Members

All you need to do here is ask for how many terms they want to renew for.

Note: We can only accept multi-term renewals in cash.
[Them]
I want to renew my membership
[You]
How many terms do you want to renew for?
[ Integer > 0 ] [ Never mind ]

Next, you have to take payment.

3. Payment

We cannot process card payments. So, only cash can be taken in the office (and at events).
Please do not mix money between the different cups (Membership is the blue cup, Swag is the red one). If you need to make change, do it within the same cup that the money is going into.

There are 3 cups where payment goes into:

  1. Membership (A blue cup with a lid)
  2. Swag (A red solo cup)
  3. Pop/Snacks (A cup inside the fridge)

You must put the money into the correct cup for whichever purhcase they are making. (i.e. If they give you a $20 bill for swag and a membership, you must split the money correctly).

3.1. Alternative Forms of Payment

  • MathSoc Office: They can go the MathSoc office to purchase CSC Stuff (i.e. Membership and Swag).

    Note: We can only accept multi-term renewals in cash.

    THEY MUST bring back the reciept as proof, and you can then sign them up. The Reciept goes inside of the cup for whatever they are buying (Membership or Swag).

  • WUSA Store: Coming SoonTM

Once you have taken payment, do not let the person go! You need to fill in their name and quest ID if they're buying swag, and you need their quest ID for processing their membership on ceo.

4. Ceo

To sign people up, or renew their memberships, or checking their membership status, you must first login to the csc servers, and run ceo. 

ssh <my-watiam-id>@hfcs.csclub.uwaterloo.ca
# once logged in
ceo

It will prompt you for your CSC password. Once in ceo, it'll look like this: 

                            CSC Electronic Office

           Members   < Add user                                            >
                     < Renew user                                          >
                     < Get user info                                       >
                     < Reset password                                      >
                     < Change login shell                                  >

            Groups   < Add group                                           >
                     < Get group members                                   >
                     < Add member to group                                 >
                     < Remove member from group                            >

         Databases   < Create database                                     >
                     < Reset database password                             >

         Positions   < Get positions                                       >
                     < Set positions                                       >





Press q or Esc to quit

To control, use your arrow keys, and press enter to select items. You can also just type into fields that need to be filled.

As office staff, you do not need to worry about most of these things, you will usually only need to touch:

  • Add user
  • Renew user
  • Get user info

4.1. Add/Renew User

Once in these menus, all you need to do is fill in the [ Username ] field, and the [ Number of Terms ] field. The rest are filled in for you.

Note on Membership Type: If someone asks about club reps, just tell them to email syscom, unless you know how this works.

Make sure that you have fully completed signup/renewal before letting the person go (in case anything goes wrong).

4.2. Club Reps

CSC provides services to other clubs at Waterloo. Thus, they need a representative from their club to have access to CSClub services so that they can manage their Club's stuff on CSC servers.

Note: Any inquiries regarding Club Reps should be handled by Syscom, so tell them to send an email to Syscom.

5. Swag Purchases

If they are buying swag, make sure to fill out the swag purchases form before they leave. You will need their WATIAM ID (quest username) and their name.

If they bought multiple items, you will need to fill out the form once for each item they bought. I know this sucks, but that's just how it is. Sorry.
