2.1. Thunderbolt Docks

Exposition
Students frequently bring their own laptops to work in the CSC office. Thunderbolt docks enable people to

  1. Charge their laptop
  2. Use keyboards/mice at the office terminals
  3. Use the monitors by the office terminals
  4. Access the school network (wireless signal is poor in the office)

using one cable.

Termcom also benefits from this as people stop unplugging stuff from the office terminals and leaving a disaster of unplugged cables on the desk.

Requested Budget: $904
I am asking for enough budget to cover the cost of two more thunderbolt docks. Each dock costs $399 on Amazon, and after tax the combined cost will be around $904.

We are excited to present awards in various categories such as programming, web development, cybersecurity, and more.
